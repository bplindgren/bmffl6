Bowery Maddness Fantasy Football League Homepage

This site serves as a statistics portal and historical record for the BMFFL (2011-2019)

The main features of the site are:

  • A scoreboard that displays the current week's games. The drop down menus switch the scoreboard to a different week
  • Owner information, including all-time stats and a list of teams
  • Season information, including a list of champions
  • Team Information, including season stats and a list of games with results
  • Matchup Information: Choose any two owners to see a list of their head-to-head matchups and a comparison of their all-time stats
  • All-time standings: lifetime stats for all owners
  • A smackboard where owners and fans of the BMFFL can talk some smack (you need to create an account to create/update/delete posts on the smackboard)
